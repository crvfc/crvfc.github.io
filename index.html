Green Valley North Turkey Trot

We invite our friends and neighbors to join us for the 33rd annual Green Valley North Turkey Trot to benefit children battling cancer and life-threatening illness, the Lenny "Batman" Robinson Hope for Henry at Sinai Lifebridge and the Chestnut Ridge Volunteer Fire Company.

This annual community fundraising event is under new management for 2016 and kicks off Thanksgiving Morning, Thursday, November 24th at 8am. Join us for a 5-mile run and 3-mile family walk followed by post-trot festivities. 100% of all donations are completely tax deductible and go directly into the hands of our beneficiaries.

Hope for Henry is reinventing how hospitals care for seriously ill children and their families through innovative programs that entertain, reduce stress and empower children to be active participants in their own care.

How to Register

Online Registration

To register online, please complete the online registration form and make your donation securely using PayPal.

Pre-Registration

Beat the lines on Thanksgiving Morning by attending the pre-registration night on November 22, 2016 from 5:00 PM until 8:00 PM. Bring this form, your donation (check, cash, charge or PayPal) and a canned food donation to the Chestnut Ridge Volunteer Fire Company (12020 Greenspring Avenue, Owings Mills, MD 21117)

Race Day Registration

Bring this form, your donation (check, cash, charge or PayPal) and a canned food donation to the registration tent on the morning of the race.

All Registrants may pickup their turkey trot shirt at the pre-registration night or at the registration tent on the morning of the race. Availability and size requests while supplies last.
